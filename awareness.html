Digital Defense Guide

Simple steps to protect your digital life from cyber threats.

1. Use Strong Passwords

Don't use your name or birthday. Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols like @#$!

2. Enable Two-Factor (2FA)

Turn on 2FA for WhatsApp, Facebook, and Gmail. Even if someone knows your password, they can't log in without the code.

Cyber Safety Cheat Sheet

DO THIS

  • Check the sender's email address carefully.
  • Report suspicious activities to CCIU.
  • Lock your phone with a PIN or Fingerprint.

DON'T DO THIS

  • Click on links in random SMS messages.
  • Use public Wi-Fi for bank transactions.
  • Share your OTP with anyone.
