Trending Cyber Scams

Stay alert! These are the most common scams reported in Sri Lanka recently.

High Risk

WhatsApp Hijacking

Scammers ask for a 6-digit verification code sent to your phone to take over your account.

Safety Tip: Never share your OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be a friend.

Medium Risk

Fake Telegram Jobs

Offers high-paying simple tasks (like liking YouTube videos) and then asks for "investment" money.

Safety Tip: Real companies will never ask you to pay money to start working.

High Risk

Bank Call Scams

Fake calls claiming to be from your bank asking for CVV or Internet Banking passwords.

Safety Tip: Banks will never call you to ask for your password or PIN.
